About College

Mandavya First Grade College Of Science & Commerce. Jnanasagara Campus, M.c. Road, Mandya Was Started In 2006. The College Is Permanent Affiliated With The University Of Mysore & Recognized By The Govt Of Karnataka. Within A Couple Of Years Of Its Inception, The College Has Gained Popularity In The Southern Part Of Karnataka.


The College Is Located At Jnanasagara Campus, M.c. Road, Nh 275 In The Outskirts Of Mandya City. About 4 Km. From The Mandya Ksrtc Bus Stand The College Building Is An Architectural Beauty Situated Amidst Beautiful Gardens And Laws.


The College Is Creating The Needs Of The Students By Providing All The Modern Facilities Like Highly Furnished Class Rooms, State Of The Art Equipped, A Spacious Library With A Reference Section, An Internet Facility, And Above All Highly Qualified, Experienced Dedicated Teaching Faculty. To Encourage The All-round Development Of The Students, The College Is Providing Plenty Of Time & Space For Indoor Games, Athletics & Other Outdoor Games.
